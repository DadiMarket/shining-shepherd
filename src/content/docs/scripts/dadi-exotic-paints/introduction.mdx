# DADI EXOTIC PAINTS [ESX] [V1.1.2]

Discover the revolutionary vehicle customization experience with **Dadi Exotic Paints** in Dadi Market! This exclusive ESX script allows you to take the look and feel of your vehicles to a whole new level.

The Dadi Paints script offers a unique vehicle customization experience on your FiveM server. This script allows you to take the appearance and style of your vehicles to an entirely new level.

## Key Features

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Chameleon Paints" icon="fill">
    With over 80 chameleon paints, vehicles can instantly change color based on the surrounding light, creating stunning iridescent effects.

  <Card title="Special Effects" icon="sun">

## What's Inside?

The script includes all the necessary files for a complete chameleon paint experience:

- **Custom Meta Files**: Includes generated `carcols.meta` and `carmodcols.meta` for high-quality visuals.
- **Embedded Assets**: Optimized `.ytd` files for smooth performance.
- **Modular Code**: Clean `client.lua` and `server.lua` logic.
